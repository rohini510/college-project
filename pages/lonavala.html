Lonavala hill station The place of peace.....

Lonavala is a hill station in the Pune district, Maharashtra, India.

The Great History of Lonavala:-

  • The region of Lonavala was ruled by the Maratha clans and the Peshwas. Sardar Dabhade, the general of the Peshwa warriors used to watch over the coming intruders from the hills of Lonavala.
  • In 1778, when the British explored the Maharashtra region, they bravely stood their fort against them.
  • Back then, Lonavala had nothing except cliffs and hills full covered in a green sheet of forests
  • Lonavala had nothing except cliffs and hills full covered in a green sheet of forests

Why to visitgit....?:-

  • The lonavalais a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of India's rich cultural heritage.
  • Stunning Photography: the lonavala offers incredible photo opportunities.
  • It's a great opportunity to immerse oneself in Indian culture.
  • The surrounding gardens and the reflection pool add to the visual charm.

Best Time to Visit:-

  • >The best time to visit lovala is in the winter season between November to March..
  • This weather is perfect for sightseeing..
  • Another good time to visit lonavala is between August and October to witness the lonavala at its best.
  • Avoid visits during Summers..
